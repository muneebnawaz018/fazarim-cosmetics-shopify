# Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Fazarim Cosmetics collects when you visit or buy from **fazarim.com**, why we collect it, and what we do with it.

## What we collect

### When you browse

Our store runs on Shopify. When you visit, we automatically receive:

- Your IP address, browser type, and time zone
- Which pages you viewed, and how long you spent on them
- The site or search that referred you to us
- Cookies placed on your device

### When you buy

To process your order, we collect:

- Your name, delivery address, and billing address
- Your email address and phone number
- Your payment details

**We never see or store your full card number.** Payment details go directly to our payment provider, which is certified to handle them.

### When you contact us

If you email us or fill in a form, we keep that correspondence and any information in it.

## Why we collect it

- To fulfil your order — to take payment, deliver your parcel, and send you order updates
- To screen orders for fraud
- To answer your questions
- To improve the store, by understanding which products and pages people look at
- To send marketing emails, **only if you have opted in**

## Who we share it with

**Shopify** — our store platform, which hosts the site and processes orders. Their privacy policy: `https://www.shopify.com/legal/privacy`

**Our courier** — [COURIER NAME], to deliver your parcel. They receive your name, address, and phone number.

**Our payment provider** — [GATEWAY NAME], to take payment.

We may also disclose information if we are legally required to, or to protect our rights.

**We do not sell your personal information.**

## Marketing

If you opt in, we'll email you about new products and offers. Every email has an unsubscribe link, and you can also email **ceo@fazarim.com** to be removed at any time.

We don't email you marketing unless you asked for it.

## Cookies

We use cookies to keep your cart working between pages, remember you if you have an account, and understand how the store is used.

You can block cookies in your browser settings. If you do, the cart may not work.

## How long we keep your information

We keep order records for as long as we need them to run the business and meet legal and tax obligations. If you ask us to delete your account, we will — but we may keep order records where the law requires it.

## Your rights

You can ask us to:

- Tell you what personal information we hold about you
- Correct anything that's wrong
- Delete your information, where we're not legally required to keep it

Email **ceo@fazarim.com** and we'll respond.

## Children

Our store is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.
