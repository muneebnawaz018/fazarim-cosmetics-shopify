# Refund Policy

We want you to be happy with what you buy from Fazarim Cosmetics. If something isn't right, here's how returns work.

## Returns window

You have **7 days** from the day your order arrives to request a return.

## What we can accept

To be eligible, the item must be:

- **Unopened and unused**, with its seal intact
- In its original packaging
- Accompanied by proof of purchase (order number or receipt)

## What we cannot accept

For hygiene and safety reasons, we **cannot accept returns on opened or used cosmetics**. This includes any product whose seal has been broken, whose applicator has been used, or which has been swatched or tested.

This applies to lipsticks, foundations, concealers, mascaras, serums, creams, and every other item we sell. Once a cosmetic product has touched skin, we cannot resell it, and we will not resell it to someone else.

We also cannot accept:

- Sale or clearance items, unless faulty
- Gift cards
- Items returned more than 7 days after delivery

## Damaged, faulty, or wrong items

Check your order as soon as it arrives. If an item is damaged, defective, or not what you ordered, contact us **within 48 hours of delivery** with photographs of the item and its packaging.

We will replace it or refund it in full, including delivery charges, and we will pay for return postage. This is our mistake, not yours.

## How to start a return

Email **ceo@fazarim.com** with:

- Your order number
- What you're returning and why
- Photographs, if the item is damaged or faulty

Please don't send anything back before you hear from us — we need to confirm the return is eligible and give you a return address.

## Return postage

If you're returning an item because you changed your mind, **you pay the return postage**, and the original Rs 250 delivery charge is not refunded.

If the item was faulty, damaged, or wrong, **we pay**.

## Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll email you to confirm whether it was approved.

If approved, your refund is issued to your original payment method within **[NUMBER] business days**. Depending on your bank, it can take a further few days to appear on your statement.

**If you paid Cash on Delivery**, we cannot refund automatically. We'll arrange a bank transfer, and we'll need your account details to do it. [CONFIRM: bank transfer / Easypaisa / JazzCash]

## Exchanges

The fastest way to exchange something is to return the original item for a refund, then place a new order.
