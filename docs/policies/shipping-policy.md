# Shipping Policy

## Where we deliver

We currently deliver **within Pakistan only**. We do not ship internationally.

## Delivery charges

| Order total | Delivery charge |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Under Rs 5,000 | **Rs 250** |
| Rs 5,000 and above | **Free** |

The free-delivery threshold is calculated on your product subtotal, before delivery charges are added.

## Delivery time

Orders are dispatched within **[NUMBER] business days** of being placed. Once dispatched, delivery takes **3 to 5 business days**.

Delivery times are estimates, not guarantees. Weather, public holidays, and courier delays can all add time. During sales and festive periods, dispatch may take longer than usual.

## Order processing

We process orders Monday to [DAY], excluding public holidays. An order placed on a Friday evening will be processed the following working day.

You'll receive a confirmation email when your order is placed, and a second email with tracking details once it's dispatched.

## Tracking your order

Your dispatch email includes a tracking number for [COURIER NAME]. Tracking information can take up to 24 hours to become active after dispatch.

## If your order hasn't arrived

If your tracking shows the parcel as delivered but you haven't received it, please:

1. Check with others at your delivery address
2. Check any safe place or reception the courier may have used
3. Contact us at **ceo@fazarim.com** with your order number

Please allow the full estimated delivery window to pass before reporting an order as missing.

## Incorrect addresses

Please check your delivery address carefully before placing your order. We are not responsible for orders delivered to an address you entered incorrectly, and we cannot redirect a parcel once it has been dispatched.

If a parcel is returned to us because the address was wrong or nobody was available to receive it, we can re-send it once you've paid the delivery charge again.

## Damaged parcels

If your parcel arrives visibly damaged, photograph it **before opening**, and contact us within 48 hours. See our Refund Policy for what happens next.
