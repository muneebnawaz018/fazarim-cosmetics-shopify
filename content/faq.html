About Fazarim

What is FAZARIM?

FAZARIM is a Pakistani skincare and personal care brand dedicated to providing high-quality products for healthy skin and hair. Our product range includes soaps, shampoos, serums, lotions, and creams designed for everyday care.

Where are FAZARIM products made?

Our products are manufactured under strict quality standards using carefully selected ingredients to ensure safety, effectiveness, and consistency.

Are your products suitable for all skin types?

Many of our products are formulated for everyday use and are suitable for most skin types. Please read the product description and usage instructions before purchasing. If you have sensitive skin or a medical condition, we recommend consulting a dermatologist before use.

Are your products original?

Yes. Every product purchased through the official FAZARIM website is 100% genuine and quality assured.

Ordering

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our website, select your desired products, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Fill in your shipping details, choose your preferred payment method, and confirm your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently offer:

  • Cash on Delivery (COD)
  • Debit & Credit Cards
  • Online Bank Transfer
  • Mobile Wallets (where available)

Available payment options will be displayed during checkout.

Do you offer Cash on Delivery (COD)?

Yes. We offer Cash on Delivery (COD) across most cities in Pakistan. Simply select COD during checkout and pay when your order is delivered.

Can I change or cancel my order?

You may request changes or cancellation before your order has been dispatched. Please contact our customer support team as soon as possible for assistance.

Delivery

Do you deliver all over Pakistan?

Yes, we deliver to most cities and towns across Pakistan through our trusted courier partners.

How long does delivery take?

Estimated delivery times are:

  • Major Cities: 2–4 business days
  • Other Areas: 3–7 business days

Delivery times may vary during holidays, promotional campaigns, or due to unforeseen courier delays.

How much is the delivery charge?

Standard delivery is Rs. 250. Orders over Rs. 10,000 ship free. Shipping is calculated at checkout based on your delivery location, and any free shipping offer will be shown there.

Can I track my order?

Yes. Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via SMS or email so you can monitor your shipment.

Returns

What if I receive a damaged or incorrect product?

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, please contact our customer support within 48 hours of delivery with your order number and clear photos. We will review your request and provide an appropriate solution.

Do you accept returns or exchanges?

Returns and exchanges are accepted only for damaged, defective, or incorrectly delivered products, subject to our Return & Exchange Policy. Products must be unused and in their original packaging.

Do you offer discounts or promotions?
